CodeBlue is a a non-profit organization sponsored by the University of Michigan Urologic Oncology Department. We're dedicated to raising both funds and awareness for prostate cancer research here at U of M.


Our club provides students with the opportunity to participate in engaging and entertaining activities, in order to raise money for this research. We strive to aid in the fight against prostate cancer and provide students on campus with contemporary fundraising methods. Our goal is to have fun and support a great cause.
