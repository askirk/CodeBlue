Mission Statement

CodeBlue is a non-profit organization at the University of Michigan dedicated to the awareness and continued research of prostate cancer. All proceeds generated by various fundraising events are donated directly to the Prostate Cancer Foundation.


CodeBlue provides students with the opportunity to participate in engaging and entertaining activities, in order to raise money for our designated prostate cancer research foundation. We strive to aid in the fight against prostate cancer and provide students on campus with contemporary fundraising methods. Our goal is to have fun and support a great cause.
